avatar

Avatar is a 2009 American epic science fiction film directed, written, co-produced, and co-edited by James Cameron. Set in the mid-22nd century, the film follows a paraplegic former Marine who is dispatched to the moon Pandora on a unique mission, but becomes torn between following his orders and protecting the world he feels is his home.
